Jumu'ah Announcement

Unfortunately, due to the current situation we have had to significantly reduce the capacity for Jumu’ah prayers down to 150 spaces in each prayer. Please do not travel to the Masjid if you have not registered.

1) There will be three Jumu’ah khutbahs performed:

12.30pm – Shaykh Ahsan Hanif; 1.15pm – Shaykh Hafeezullah Khan; 2.00pm – Shaykh Mustafa Abu Rayyan

2) The khutbah and salah will be shortened and will not exceed 20 minutes in total.

DO NOT attend if you are unwell or have symptoms of COVID-19.

All khutbahs will be posted later in the afternoon on our YouTube & Facebook pages InshaAllah.
